Able Player with Video Transcript Sorter (VTS)

To help authors/developers achieve synchronization between all timed text files, Able Player includes a Video Transcript Sorter (VTS). The VTS displays all timed text content from all sources in a table, and provides several features that enable users to rearrange content and modify start and end times.

Users can also insert new content into the table, which can be useful for authoring low frequency content such as chapters and description. To use VTS, add <div id="able-vts"></div> to your HTML page.
